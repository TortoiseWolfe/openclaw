# NPC Templates -- Star Wars d6

Recurring NPCs and quick-reference stat blocks for the GM.

## Imperial Forces

### Stormtrooper (generic)
- Dex 2D, Kno 2D, Mec 2D, Per 2D, Str 2D, Tec 2D
- Skills: Blaster 4D, Brawling Parry 4D, Dodge 3D
- Armor: Stormtrooper armor (+1D physical, +1 energy)
- Weapons: E-11 blaster rifle (5D damage)
- Tactics: Move in groups of 4. One covers, three advance. Not creative.

### Stormtrooper Sergeant
- Dex 2D+2, Kno 2D, Mec 2D, Per 3D, Str 2D+1, Tec 2D
- Skills: Blaster 5D, Command 4D, Dodge 4D, Brawling 3D+2
- Weapons: E-11 blaster rifle, thermal detonator (7D/5D/3D)
- Tactics: Actually competent. Flanks, uses cover, calls reinforcements.

### Imperial Officer (junior)
- Dex 2D, Kno 3D, Mec 2D+1, Per 3D, Str 2D, Tec 2D+1
- Skills: Bureaucracy 4D, Command 4D, Blaster 3D, Willpower 4D
- Weapons: SE-14C blaster pistol (4D damage)
- Notes: Arrogant. Underestimates non-humans. Has authority but not street smarts.

### Scout Trooper
- Dex 3D, Kno 2D, Mec 3D+2, Per 3D, Str 2D, Tec 2D
- Skills: Blaster 4D+1, Speeder Bikes 5D, Search 4D, Sneak 3D+2
- Weapons: Scout blaster pistol (3D+2), speeder bike (mounted blaster 3D)
- Tactics: Fast recon. Hit and run. Reports to base.

## Criminal Underworld

### Bounty Hunter (competent)
- Dex 3D+1, Kno 2D+2, Mec 3D, Per 3D+1, Str 3D, Tec 2D+2
- Skills: Blaster 5D, Dodge 4D+1, Search 4D+1, Sneak 4D, Streetwise 4D, Tracking 5D
- Weapons: Heavy blaster pistol (5D), vibroblade (STR+2D)
- Gear: Tracking fob, binder cuffs, jet pack (optional)
- Tactics: Patient. Observes first. Prefers capture over kill (bounties pay more alive).

### Smuggler (typical)
- Dex 3D, Kno 2D+1, Mec 3D+2, Per 3D, Str 2D+1, Tec 3D
- Skills: Blaster 4D, Dodge 4D, Starship Piloting 5D, Con 4D, Bargain 4D, Streetwise 4D
- Weapons: DL-44 heavy blaster (5D)
- Gear: Comlink, 200-1000 credits, light freighter
- Personality: Talks big. Loyal to friends. Will sell out strangers to save themselves.

### Hutt Crime Lord
- Dex 1D, Kno 4D, Mec 2D, Per 4D+2, Str 5D, Tec 2D
- Skills: Bargain 6D, Con 5D+1, Intimidation 6D, Willpower 5D, Value 5D+1
- Weapons: None (has guards for that)
- Notes: Never fights personally. Always surrounded by 4-8 guards. Speaks through a protocol droid.

## Rebel Alliance

### Rebel Soldier
- Dex 2D+2, Kno 2D, Mec 2D, Per 2D+1, Str 2D+2, Tec 2D
- Skills: Blaster 4D, Dodge 3D+2, Survival 3D, First Aid 3D
- Weapons: A280 blaster rifle (5D+1)
- Personality: Committed to the cause. Will take risks for civilians.

### Rebel Agent
- Dex 2D+1, Kno 3D, Mec 2D+2, Per 3D+2, Str 2D, Tec 3D
- Skills: Con 4D+2, Forgery 4D, Sneak 4D, Streetwise 4D+1, Persuasion 4D, Computer Prog 4D
- Weapons: Hold-out blaster (3D+1, concealed)
- Gear: Fake IDs (multiple), encrypted comlink, credits
- Personality: Calm under pressure. Lies fluently. Genuinely believes in freedom.

## Quick NPC Generator
For improvised NPCs, use this template:
- All attributes: 2D (average person) or 3D (competent professional)
- One specialty skill: 5D (what they're known for)
- One weakness: 1D or 2D in something obvious
- Motivation: one word (greed, loyalty, revenge, survival, curiosity)
- Quirk: one detail (nervous laugh, missing eye, talks to their blaster, collects hats)
