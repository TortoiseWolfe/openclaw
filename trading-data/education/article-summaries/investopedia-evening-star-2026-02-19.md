## Key Concepts

An evening star is a stock price chart pattern that's used by technical analysts to detect when a trend is about to reverse. It's a bearish candlestick pattern that consists of three candles: a large white candlestick, a small-bodied candle, and a red candle. Evening star patterns appear at the peak of a price uptrend, signaling its end. In contrast, the morning star pattern is considered bullish.
