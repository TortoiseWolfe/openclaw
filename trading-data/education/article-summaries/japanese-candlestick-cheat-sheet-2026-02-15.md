Candlestick Anatomy Body : The real body represents the range between the opening and closing prices. Color : White/Green : Indicates the closing price was higher than the opening price (bullish). Black/Red : Indicates the closing price was lower than the opening price (bearish). Wicks (Shadows): Show the highest and lowest price points reached during the trading period.
