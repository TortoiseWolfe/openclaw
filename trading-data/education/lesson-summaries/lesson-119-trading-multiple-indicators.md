Lesson #119 Summary: Trading with Multiple Chart Indicators

Key Concepts:
- Combining indicators (e.g., Bollinger Bands + Stochastic) to filter trade signals
- Example: EUR/USD 4-hour chart showing sell signals from both indicators
- Bollinger Bands resistance level + Stochastic overbought area = potential price drop
- Importance of cross-verification to mitigate individual indicator flaws
